2 shallots, finely chopped
150ml white wine
1 bay leaf
2 tsp light soft brown sugar
2 tbsp English mustard
1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
200ml creme fraîche

Put the shallots, wine, bay leaf and sugar in a pan and set over a medium heat. Simmer until the wine has reduced by half and the shallots have softened. Stir in the 2 mustards and creme fraîche. Serve warm or cold.
